How it works

  1. 1

    Create a hunt

    Choose your game, target Pokémon and hunt method. Encounty knows the correct odds.

  2. 2

    Capture your game

    Select the game window or screen. Mark the encounter with a template, once.

  3. 3

    Hunt

    Encounters are detected and counted automatically while you play. Until it sparkles.

More features

  • No double counting

    A three-phase state machine (hysteresis, cooldown, counting) with tolerance for single shaky frames makes sure each encounter is counted exactly once.

  • All games, Gen 1-9

    Every mainline Pokémon game with game-specific shiny odds, including the Shiny Charm and accurate per-method probabilities.

  • 60+ hunt methods

    Masuda, Poké Radar, SOS chaining, DexNav, Mass Outbreaks, Sandwich hunts and many more, each with its own odds model.

  • OBS overlay editor

    Show your counters on stream: drag-and-drop overlay editor with live preview and a browser-source URL for OBS.

  • Global hotkeys

    Count, pause or correct manually from anywhere with system-wide hotkeys.

  • Template import & export

    Share your detection templates or move them between machines with one click.

  • Pokédex with sprites

    Built-in Pokédex search with multiple sprite styles for your hunts and overlays.

  • Modern architecture

    Go backend, React frontend and an Electron shell: fast, robust and actively developed.

  • Cross-platform & multi-arch

    Linux, Windows 11 and macOS, on x64 and ARM64. Coming soon to your Toaster™.

  • UI in 5 languages

    English, German, Spanish, French and Japanese.

FAQ

Was Encounty built with the help of AI?

Yes. Encounty is developed with the help of LLM coding assistants. A free application of this scope would simply not be possible as a solo project without them. Every change is reviewed, covered by automated tests and CI, and the complete source code is open on GitHub.

Is Encounty really free?

Yes, completely. No ads, no pro tier, no paywall. Encounty is a hobby project, free under the AGPL-3.0 license.

Do I need an account or an internet connection?

No. Encounty runs entirely on your machine and works offline. Internet is only used for optional things like app updates and sprite downloads. Your hunts stay local.

Can using Encounty get me banned?

Encounty only watches: it reads your screen or capture card, never touches the game, sends no inputs and automates nothing. It is a smarter tally counter, not a cheat tool.

Which games and setups work?

Anything you can show on your screen: PC releases or a console through a capture card. Hunt tracking covers every mainline game from Gen 1 to 9, and auto-detection works wherever an encounter is visually recognizable.

Where does the odds data come from?

The odds and method data come from a community research thread on r/pokemon that documents the hunting methods of every game; the app credits this source under Settings, About. Read the research thread.

How much performance does the detection cost?

Very little. Matching runs as WebGPU compute shaders on your GPU with adaptive polling, so the CPU stays free for your game. Systems without WebGPU fall back to a CPU pipeline automatically. An integrated GPU is enough.
